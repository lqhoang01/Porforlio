01WHO I AM

Half engineer, half people-person

A Software Engineering student based in Ho Chi Minh City who's spent the last few years moving between web development, client-facing consulting, and more recently, AI training. I care equally about how something is built and how it's explained to the person who needs it.

02JOURNEY

Built skills the hands-on way

I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, then picked up WordPress and Webflow while consulting on websites at Nasani. In parallel, I worked in insurance and sales at Prudential, where I also coordinated a small marketing team. Different rooms, same instinct: figure out what's actually needed, then go build it.

03CURRENT FOCUS

Web projects by day, AI training on the side

Right now I split my time between freelance web projects for international clients and AI voice training work with Volga Partners and Sharework AI. Both push me to be precise — one with code, the other with language and data quality.

04CAREER GOAL

Looking for where code meets consulting

I want to grow into a role where software development and client-facing consulting aren't separate skill sets — somewhere that values both technical thinking and the ability to actually talk to people about what they need.

05WORKING STYLE

Led people before I led projects

Nine years coaching a competitive cheerleading team taught me how to read a room, set clear expectations, and stay composed under pressure — long before I had a project to manage. I bring the same approach now: listen first, communicate clearly, stay accountable. Comfortable working remotely across time zones, and still actively sharpening my English and my stack.
